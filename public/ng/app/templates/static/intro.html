Welcome to Pain Squad+

You've been enlisted in an elite group known as Pain Squad. Your job is to monitor your pain and report clues in the cases.

File pain cases

Your phone will alert you in the morning and evening when a pain case is due. You can also file a case any other time you feel pain.

Get Advice

After each case has been filed, we will send you advice you can use to manage your pain. Your healthcare team will also be watching your pain and may contact you to help.

Earn Awards

If you file enough cases, you will move up in ranking and earn special awards!
